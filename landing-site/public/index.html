FAQ

The straight answers.

Have a question we didn't cover? Email support and a human will respond.

What counts as one credit?

One credit gets you about 15–30 high-quality, scored leads. Costs can scale based on your search radius — a tighter area is more efficient, while a wider sweep uses more. Once results are delivered, you can filter, sort, and revisit them as much as you want without spending another credit.

How is the scoring actually calculated?

Our AI analyzes dozens of real-time signals — review volume, rating, claimed-listing status, photo count, business hours, and more — to produce a Legitimacy score. Then it cross-references website presence, web health, SEO performance, and legitimacy to generate an Opportunity score. The AI weighs each factor dynamically so you always see the most relevant leads first. Every point is fully attributed inside the app — no black boxes, no guesswork.

Where does the data come from?

Google Business Profile data, live web crawls of each business's linked website (when one exists), and public signals like HTTPS status, page performance, and SEO metadata. We verify "no website" independently — not just absence from GMB.

Can I avoid paying for the same leads twice?

Yes. On Pro and Agency plans, leads you've saved are automatically excluded from future searches, so you don't burn credits re-discovering businesses you've already contacted.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. The Free tier gives you 3 searches with full scoring and diagnostics — no credit card required. See real leads, real scores, and real diagnostics before you spend a dime. When you're ready to run a real pipeline, plans start at $19/mo.

What makes this different from other lead tools?

Most tools give you a list of names and phone numbers. FieldStack gives you scored, qualified leads with full reasoning — you know exactly why each business is worth calling before you pick up the phone. It's built specifically for web developers and agencies selling websites, not generic sales teams.

Does this replace my CRM?

Not yet — and that's by design. FieldStack handles discovery and qualification so you spend zero time prospecting. Save leads, track their status (new / contacted / negotiating / won / lost), and export to your CRM when ready. Native integrations are on the roadmap.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If FieldStack isn't paying for itself in new clients, you can cancel in two clicks. But most users find that a single closed deal covers months of their subscription.
